Carved into the stone floor, ancient words read:

  ⚠  WARNING  ⚠

  "Not all paths lead forward.
   The dungeon has dead ends and traps.
   But every dead end has a message.
   Explore EVERYTHING — even wrong turns teach you something."

  "And remember: if you ever get lost,
   type 'pwd' to see exactly where you are."

(pwd = "print working directory" — it shows your current location)
