The book is old, leather-bound, titled:

  "A GUIDE TO THE DEPTHS BELOW"

Most pages are destroyed, but one passage is legible:

  "The passage to the treasure lies behind door-3,
   hidden from those who only look on the surface.

   Know this: just as FILES can be hidden with a dot,
   so too can entire ROOMS be hidden.

   A directory that starts with a dot is invisible
   to those who do not know to look."

That's a big hint. Hidden DIRECTORIES exist too.
Remember this when you get to door-3.

One more line, barely legible at the bottom of the page:

  "Legend speaks of a treasure that SINGS
   to those who find it."
